Frequently asked questions
Getting Started4 questions
What is Study Buddy?
Study Buddy is a platform that connects students with study partners, provides live session tools — video, whiteboard, and shared notepad — and lets creators publish study materials like notes, quizzes, and guides.
Is it free to join?
Yes — creating an account is completely free. Some creator content may be paid, but the core platform features are always free.
How do I find study partners?
Go to Find Buddies and filter by subject, schedule, and study style. You can send match requests directly from there.
What subjects are supported?
We support a wide range across CS, IT, Multimedia, Business, Engineering, Health Sciences, and Social Sciences. Set your subjects in your profile to get matched and see targeted content.
Account & Profile3 questions
How do I update my subjects?
Go to your profile and click Edit Profile. Use the subject picker to search and add up to 5 subjects. Your subjects determine which study ads and partners you see.
Can I reset my password?
Password reset via email is coming soon. For now, contact our support team via the Contact page if you're locked out.
How do I delete my account?
Send us a message via the Contact page and our team will handle the deletion within 24 hours.
Creator Hub3 questions
How do I become a creator?
Apply through the Creator Hub section after logging in. Once approved, you can publish notes, quizzes, guides, templates, and flashcards.
Do products need a subject tag?
Yes — a subject tag is required when creating a product. It makes your content discoverable by students with matching subjects and eligible for ad rotation.
How do I earn from my content?
You can set a price on your products and quizzes. Payment processing integration is currently in development.
Sessions & Matching2 questions
How do live sessions work?
Sessions include video calling, a shared whiteboard, and a collaborative notepad — all in one room. Create or join a room from the Sessions tab after logging in.
Can I lock a session with a PIN?
Yes — when creating a room you can set a 4-digit PIN. Only people with the PIN can join the session.
